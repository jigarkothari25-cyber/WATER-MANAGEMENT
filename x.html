Smart Water-Saving Tips

Daily Water Conservation Tips

Turn off the tap while brushing—save up to 20 liters daily.
Take shorter showers (5 minutes max) to reduce water waste.
Prefer bucket bathing instead of showers to save 40–60 liters.
Fix leaking taps immediately—one drop per second wastes 1,000 liters/month.
Use leftover RO water for plants and cleaning floors.
Reuse cooking water for soups, watering plants, or cleaning utensils.
Store cold water in the fridge instead of running the tap to cool it.
Install small rainwater collectors on your balcony or terrace.
Use minimal detergent—less rinsing means less water used.
Water plants early morning to reduce evaporation.
